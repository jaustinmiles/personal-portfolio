Intro

I'm a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech, where I specialize in Graphics and Modeling/Simulation. Although I currently live in Atlanta, I come from a log cabin in a small town known as Ball Ground, GA. Outside of school, I enjoy lifting weights, playing guitar, throwing frisbee, and just hanging out.

Professional Interests

In my time at Georgia Tech, I've become deeply invested in one of my concentrations: computer graphics and media. I've also developed a passion for developing personal projects and working on large scale systems. Outside of computer science, I've found it extremely rewarding to help others through mentoring hackathons and serving as a Teaching Assistant to an intro Computer Science classes.


Here are some of the topics that interest me:

  • 2D/3D Transformation
  • Ray Tracing
  • Character Animation
  • GPU Programming
  • Shadows
  • ... and many more!

Personal Interests

Though academics and professional development are extremely important to me, I enjoy spending time outside of school and work focusing on my hobbies. I have a vested interest in music, and enjoy playing electric guitar in a band with two of my closest friends. I also prioritize fitness and enjoy working out and staying active, even if it's just to go throw a frisbee or play basketball with some friends. I also enjoy playing video games and keeping up to date with all the amazing things happening in the video games and graphics industries. Reading fantasy novels is another hobby of mine. Currently, I'm 5 books deep into the Wheel of Time. Outside of all this, I enjoy exploring Atlanta with my friends.
